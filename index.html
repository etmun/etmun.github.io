What is MUN?

Model United Nations is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students play the role of delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real world issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned country.

Contact Us

This Year's Theme: Reminiscence of Peace

In order to solve issues of the present and future, we must look back at the past and Reminisce on its events to find effective solutions, in order to make the world a better place. This theme is all about connecting between the past, present and the future in a global context in order to shed light on recurring and new issues.
